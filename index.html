How to Play

×
  • Goal: Satisfy customers before they leave!
  • Constraints: Each customer wants a specific letter in a specific spot (e.g., Starts with 'A').
  • Economy: Buying letters raises their price (Inflation). Waiting lowers prices (Deflation).
  • Combos: Try to satisfy 2 or 3 customers with a single word to maximize profit!
  • Game Over: If a customer waits too long (0 Hearts), they leave and the slot is DESTROYED.

Tip: Don't spend more than you earn!
