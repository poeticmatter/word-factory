Word Shop

Cash: $0.00
Turn: 0

HOW TO PLAY

×
  • Fill Orders: Build words that match customer requests (e.g., "Starts with A").
  • The Market: Buying letters spikes the cost. Waiting drops it. Buy low!
  • Combos: Satisfy 2+ orders with one word. You buy the letters once, but get paid by everyone!
  • Survival: Customers lose Patience over time. If they run out, they leave a bad review.

Game Over if you lose all seats or run out of cash!
