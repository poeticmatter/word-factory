HOW TO PLAY

×
  • Fill Orders: Customers need specific letters in specific spots. (e.g. If Customer 1 wants 'A', your word must start with 'A').
  • Beat the Boss: Critics appear periodically with a secret word. Guess words to find clues (Yellow letters). Deduce their secret word to defeat them!
  • Stay Alive: Customers lose Patience over time. If they run out, they leave a bad review.
  • Combos: Clear multiple customers with a single word to save turns.

Game Over if you lose all seats!
